Visual Hierarchy

Ford Tough

This picture is a good example of Visual Hierarchy. This Vehicle has the "scaled" up illustration. The image all around the vehicle is slightly blurred as well. I do feel that a picture next to a big body of water really gives the depth effet to any object in a given picture.

Repetition

Samsung is the best

This picture possesses a very visible repetition effect. It displays the phones in a repetitious pattern. Obviously the circular pattern but also the color pattern as well as it repeats throughout the circle.

Nike - Just Do It!

You could tell the model in this photo hated the photographer by the way thier foot is perfectly aligned with one another. Though it isnt a straight horizontal alignment, the angled alignment plays well with each other
